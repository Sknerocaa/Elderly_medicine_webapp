Health Tips for Seniors

Simple habits for a healthier life

  • 1 Drink boiled or filtered water regularly to stay hydrated.
  • 2 Walk for 20-30 minutes every morning or evening.
  • 3 Check your blood pressure and sugar levels weekly.
  • 4 Eat fresh fruits and green leafy vegetables daily.
  • 5 Take your prescribed medicines on time. Never skip a dose.
  • 6 Get 7-8 hours of good sleep every night.
  • 7 Do simple yoga or stretching exercises for joint flexibility.
  • 8 Reduce salt and sugar intake in your meals.
  • 9 Keep emergency contact numbers saved on your phone.
  • 10 Stay happy! Talk to your family and friends often.
